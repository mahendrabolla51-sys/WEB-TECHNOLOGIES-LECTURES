How SGPA & CGPA are calculated (example)

SGPA = (Sum of (Grade points × Credits)) / (Sum of Credits) for the semester.

CGPA = (Sum of Grade points × Credits across all semesters) / (Sum of credits across all semesters).

Example: If you have courses with credits 4,4 and marks resulting in grade points 9,8 respectively, SGPA = (9×4 + 8×4)/(4+4) = (36+32)/8 = 8.5
